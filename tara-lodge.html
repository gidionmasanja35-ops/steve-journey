Tarangire Safari Lodge

Home   /   Accommodations   /   Tarangire Safari Lodge Lodge

Tarangire Safari Lodge is a lovely lodge and tented camp in the north of Tarangire National Park, with some of the most amazing views of the park. The safari tents, with thatched roofs and mesh-screened windows, welcome you to rest comfortably after your game drive. For more space, you can opt for a circular bungalow, built in a similar style to the Masai bomas. All tents and bungalows have their own ensuite bathroom with solar-heated showers and 24-hour electricity. Whether you rest on your terrace, have a dip in the pool, enjoy dinner in the open-air dining room or sip a drink at the bar and lounge area, that’s all coming with stunning panoramic views.
